Success Case
An agency has trouble finding what was the level of investment of a group of companies. Example, if a company started with an Angel investor how much investment was initially provided with, if later this company was able to acquired level A of investment, the agency wanted to know who provided that investment and for how much. This information was very important because the agency facilitates company the acquisitions of loans. So our work was to create a script that search the internet and an article subscription service to constantly update the investment information of every company on our database.
